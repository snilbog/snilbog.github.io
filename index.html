About Me

My name is Neal and I’m a full stack web developer, here to rescue the people from boring and bland websites. Using your favorite pages should be fun and informative, and that’s where I come in. I think outside of the box and create engaging and useful websites with Ruby on Rails, Java, CSS, Node, and JQuery to name a few examples. Personally, I have a passion for people and being happy. My happy things are the outdoors, sports (watching and participating), cars, tasty food and traveling. I have recently moved back from spending time in Western Europe!
